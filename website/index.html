Aaron Meese, developer

Coupon Booked started as a gift idea.

Aaron's girlfriend wanted a special gift for Christmas one year, something unique. He decided to make her a virtual coupon book that sent him a text whenever she redeemed something. That way he didn't have to guess at what she wanted all the time.

She loved it so much that she showed it off to all her friends, who then begged to have Aaron teach their boyfriends how to do something similar. From there the idea just took on a life of its own, and now it has become something much bigger than one gift.

Now Coupon Booked allows many people to create personalized gifts for their loved ones, as unique and special as the person receiving them.
